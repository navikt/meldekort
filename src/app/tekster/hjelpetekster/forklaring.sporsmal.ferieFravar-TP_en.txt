Click  <strong>Yes</strong> if other reasons than your own illness have made you unable to participate in employment schemes. By measures we mean labour market measures such as an AMO course or a Job Club.
<br>
<br>
<p style="color:#ee5522;">
    <b>
        Tick No if you are unable to participate in activities due to contagion or as a result of contagion-preventing measures.
    </b>
</p>
<br>
Click <strong>Yes</strong> if you wish to be unavailable for NAV during days without any agreed activities.
<br>
<br>
Click <strong>Yes</strong> if you stay abroad. You are not entitled to benefits while participating in employment schemes unless NAV has previously stated that you are covered by one of the exceptions from the main rule.
<br>
<br>
Absence normally leads to reduced benefits.
<br>
<br>
If your absence is due to a job interview, appointment with public support services or special personal reasons, NAV can consider whether you may still receive the benefit. Please contact NAV on the first day of absence.
<br>
<br>
If you have not been absent or unavailable, click <strong>No</strong>.
