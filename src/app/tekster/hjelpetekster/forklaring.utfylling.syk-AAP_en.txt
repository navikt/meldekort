You need to register the days you have unable to take on work or perform an agreed activity due to illness.
<br>
<b><p style="color:#ee5522;">
Absence from work due to quarantine should be registered in the same way as sick leave. This does not apply when working from home.
</b></p>
<br>
This applies to days you have been too ill to participate in
<ul>
<li>measures and schemes </li>
<li>active medical treatment </li>
<li>training/education </li>
<li>other agreed activities </li>
</ul>
