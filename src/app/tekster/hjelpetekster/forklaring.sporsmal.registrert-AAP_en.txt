Click <strong> Yes</strong> if you want to receive job offers, or assistance from NAV in returning to work or assessing your ability to participate in income-generating work. 
<br>
<br>
Click <strong>No </strong>if you no longer want work assessment allowance or any other service or benefits from NAV.
<br>
<br>
If you have carried out your activity plan and you are receiving work assessment allowance while you are seeking jobs, payments will automatically cease if you answer <strong>No</strong>. You will no longer be registered with NAV and will not be assisted with job offers or labour market measures.
