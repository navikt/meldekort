Click Yes if other reasons than your own illness have made you unable to take on work, participate in employment schemes or be a job seeker. By measures we mean labour market measures such as an AMO course or a Job Club.

Click Yes if you wish to be unavailable for NAV or for taking on work during days without any agreed activities.

Click Yes if you stay abroad. You are not entitled to unemployment benefits unless NAV has previously stated that you are covered by one of the exceptions from the main rule.

Absence normally leads to reduced benefits.

Do you receive benefits while participating in employment schemes? If your absence is due to a job interview, appointment with public support services or special personal reasons, NAV can consider whether you may still receive the benefit. Please contact NAV on the first day of absence.

If you have not been absent or unavailable, click No.
