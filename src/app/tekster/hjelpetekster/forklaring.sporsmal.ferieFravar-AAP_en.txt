Click <strong>Yes </strong>if other reasons than your own illness have made you unable to take on work or participate in agreed activities.
<br><br>

<br>
This applies e.g. to Holidays. This also applies if you stay abroad without NAVs approval.
If you are a national of an EU/EEA-country and you have a temporary residence in another EU/EEA- country, you will not need approval by NAV.
<br>
<br>
Click <strong>Yes</strong> if you wish to be unavailable for NAV or for taking on work during days without any agreed activities.
<br>
<br>
Absence will as a rule reduce benefits.
<br>
<br>
NAV can consider whether you may still receive the benefit. Please contact NAV on the first day of absence.
<br>
<br>
NAV can determine whether you may continue to receive benefits, if your absence is due to special personal reasons. Contact NAV the first day of absence.
<br>
<br>
If you have not been absent or unavailable, click <strong>No</strong>.
<br>
<br>
Click <strong>No</strong> if you stay abroad and this has been preapproved by NAV.
